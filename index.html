How It Got Started

Food Processing at Our Core

For years, our group has been active in many fields — Minerals, Fabrication, Energy, Jewellery, Education and Entertainment. In 2019 we ventured into food processing with an organic soybean processing unit, exporting to USA, Canada and Europe. Building on that foundation, we established Fortifit Nutrition Pvt. Ltd. — with a clear first target: to become a leading manufacturer of Fortified Rice Kernels and help tackle malnutrition and hidden hunger across India.

Who We Are

Welcome to Fortifit

At Fortifit, we believe in nourishing communities with the power of fortified rice. Our mission is to make healthy, balanced nutrition accessible to everyone — transforming the way people eat.

Headquartered in Durg, Chhattisgarh, our extruder technology produces a diverse range of products: FRK Rice, Fryums, Pasta, Rice Crispy Balls and more.
